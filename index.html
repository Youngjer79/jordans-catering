About Jordan's Hotdogs & Mac

Our Story

Jordan's Hotdogs and Mac owner Core Spruill,started his cooking career baking pizzas at Frank Pepe's Pizzeria on Wooster Street in New Haven,CT for 17years. Corey decided to branch out and open his very own restaurant. Jordan's Hotdog & Mac first open it's doors on Sept. 3, 2012. Jordan’s is named after the owners son “He likes his hot dogs with mac and cheese. He was my inspiration,”~ Corey Spruill.

What we offer

Two classic American dishes together at last. Our signature Mac Attack hot dogs are topped with a generous helping of home-style mac & cheese for the ultimate comfort food.
Need more? We offer a full menu of gourmet hot dogs, with toppings that range from Bacon & BBQ to the chili-topped Californian. Order your catering from us for a lunch that’ll really get your guests’ attention.
