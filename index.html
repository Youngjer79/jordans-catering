Jordan's Catering is a branch Jordan's Hotdog and Mac Restaurant located in New Haven,CT. Jordan's Hot Dog and Mac is a family owned and operated business.

How Can We Serve you?

Jordan's Catering will bring the restaurant to you!

Jordan's makes it easy to order food for your next party, fundraiser or event in Connecticut. Take a look at our catering menu and place your order online!

Planning a party...

If you’re planning a get-together, the last thing you want to do is cook. With our catering packages, you won’t have to worry about planning the menu, cooking the food, and cleaning up the mess — which leaves you more time to enjoy your company!

We have a variety of packages to suit different size parties.

We’ve catered all types of events, from family reunions, corporate fundraisers, and picnics to birthday parties,wedding receptions, and more!
