Jordan's Catering

Jordan's Hotdog and Mac first open it's doors on Sept. 3, 2012, Jordan’s is named after the owners son. “He likes his hot dogs with mac and cheese. He was my inspiration,”~ Corey Spruill.


For 17 years Corey has baked pizza at Frank Pepe’s Pizzeria on Wooster Street; he began there as a teenager after eating there often with his mom, and now his mom along with his wife and father.
